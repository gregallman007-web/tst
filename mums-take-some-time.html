for new mums & their friends

Mums Take Some Time, at home.

Had a baby recently? Here's an idea. Get your mum friends together, babies and all, for a morning or an afternoon of massage — and cake. Everyone keeps asking how the little ones are doing. This is an hour or two where someone asks how you are instead.

£39.99 · per person · minimum of four mums

how it works

You gather the group, I bring everything.

While one of you has thirty minutes to yourself — candlelight, soft music, a relaxing back, neck and shoulder massage — the rest carry on chatting, with the babies in the thick of it. When your turn's done, you rejoin the group and the next mum takes her place.

If you fancy it, you can try the hot stones for the ultimate bit of relaxation, at no extra cost. It's a lovely thing to organise for an NCT group, a baby-class circle, or just the mums you've leaned on through the first few months.
