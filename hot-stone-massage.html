postnatal & non-pregnancy

Hot stone massage, brought to your door.

Some weeks you don't need anything clever — you just need the stress lifted off your shoulders. Smooth, heated stones are worked through the treatment to warm the muscles, loosen deep-set tension and leave you thoroughly unwound. One of the most relaxing treatments there is, and I bring all of it to you.

£75 · 50–60 minutes

what to expect

The warmth does the work.

The heat of the stones eases tight muscles more gently and more deeply than hands alone. I use them through a full-body treatment, adjusting the heat and pressure to what feels right for you. It runs to 50–60 minutes, and the effect tends to linger well after I've packed up and gone.

Everything comes with me — the stones, the couch, the towels and the oils. You just need a warm space and somewhere comfortable to set up.

A note on pregnancy

Hot stone massage isn't suitable during pregnancy — the combination of heat and increased circulation isn't recommended while you're expecting. So I offer it postnatally and to anyone who isn't pregnant. If you're pregnant and after some relief, my pregnancy massage is the treatment you want — and once you've had your baby and you're ready, the hot stones will be waiting.
