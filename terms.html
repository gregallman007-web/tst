the small print

Terms & conditions.

The terms below cover booking and receiving treatments with Take Some Time. They're written to be plain and fair — if anything's unclear, just ask before you book.

Last updated: June 2026

1. About us

This website is operated by Take Some Time ("we", "us", "our"), a sole-trader mobile massage practice run by Kia Welham, based near Hartford End, Essex. You can reach us at kia@takesometime.co.uk.

2. Booking & confirmation

Enquiries can be made by WhatsApp, the contact form, email or social media. A booking is only confirmed once we have agreed a date, time and treatment with you directly. We reserve the right to decline or reschedule a booking where we believe a treatment may not be safe or appropriate.

3. Health & suitability

  • Before your first treatment we'll ask you to complete a short consultation about your health, your pregnancy or recovery, and any relevant medical history.
  • You agree to give accurate information and to tell us about any changes to your health before each treatment.
  • Massage supports your wellbeing alongside your medical care — it does not replace advice or treatment from your GP, midwife or other healthcare professional.
  • Where appropriate (for example a high-risk pregnancy, recent surgery, or specific medical conditions) we may ask you to confirm your GP or midwife is happy for you to receive massage.

4. Prices & payment

Prices are as shown on the website at the time of booking and may change from time to time; the price confirmed with you at booking is the price that applies. Payment is taken as agreed at the time of your appointment unless we've arranged otherwise. A small travel or petrol fee may apply to addresses at the outer edge of our area, and we'll always confirm this before your booking is finalised.

5. Cancellations & missed appointments

  • If you need to cancel or reschedule, please give at least 24 hours' notice.
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be charged at 50% of the treatment cost.
  • Missed appointments with no notice may be charged in full.
  • If we ever need to cancel or rearrange your appointment, we'll give you as much notice as we can and offer you the earliest suitable alternative.

6. Gift vouchers

  • Vouchers are valid for six months from the date of issue unless stated otherwise.
  • Vouchers are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • Treatments booked using a voucher are subject to these same terms, including the cancellation policy.

7. Group sessions

"Mums Take Some Time" and other group bookings are arranged on a per-group basis and require a minimum number of participants. Numbers, timing and any deposit will be agreed with you when the session is booked.

8. Our space & your responsibilities

As treatments take place in your home, we ask that you provide a clean, warm and reasonably private space with room to set up a treatment couch. We treat your home and belongings with care; please let us know in advance of anything we should be aware of.

9. Liability

We hold professional and public liability insurance through the Guild of Holistic Therapists. Nothing in these terms limits or excludes our liability where it would be unlawful to do so, including liability for death or personal injury caused by our negligence. Subject to that, we are not liable for issues arising from inaccurate or incomplete health information, or from failing to follow reasonable aftercare guidance.

10. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. The version published on this page at the time of your booking is the one that applies.

11. Governing law

These terms are governed by the law of England and Wales, and any disputes are subject to the courts of England and Wales.

12. Contact

Any questions about these terms? Email kia@takesometime.co.uk or get in touch.
