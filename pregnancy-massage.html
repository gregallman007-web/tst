from 12 weeks

Pregnancy massage, in the comfort of your own home.

One of the loveliest things you can do for yourself while you're expecting — and one of the most genuinely useful. As your body changes week to week, an hour adapted to exactly where you are gives it some relief, and gives you a proper pause.

£75 · 60 minutes · from 12 weeks to just after birth

What it helps with

Made for how pregnancy actually feels.

No two pregnancies are the same, so we always start with a chat about how you're feeling. That said, the treatment tends to help most with:

  • ✓Lower back, pelvic and hip pain as your posture shifts
  • ✓Sciatica and the referred aches that come with it
  • ✓Swelling in the legs, ankles and feet
  • ✓Tension across the neck, shoulders and upper back
  • ✓Broken, restless sleep
  • ✓Anxiety, stress, and the simple need for a moment's calm
what it's like

A proper "pamper me" hour.

We start with you seated for a back, neck and shoulder massage. Then you move onto the couch — reclined against pillows, with another under your knees to take the pressure off your lower back, a bit like sitting up comfortably in bed. From there it's a foot massage, then legs, arms and hands, finishing with the scalp. By the end, most people are somewhere close to asleep.

I generally use gentle, pregnancy-safe grapeseed oil, though there are other safe oils we can talk through if you'd prefer.

When can I start?

From 12 weeks. With a high-risk pregnancy, high blood pressure or any complications, have a quick word with your midwife or GP first — you're welcome to ask me anything before you book. Massage sits alongside your medical care; it never replaces it.
