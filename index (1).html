How it works

No clinic to find. No parking. No waiting room.

Just a proper, restorative massage in your own front room — and the rare luxury of not having to go anywhere afterwards.

why a home visit

The most personal way to work.

I've worked in clinics and from home over the years, and given the choice I'll always favour treating you in your own space. There's no rushing to an appointment while you're pregnant or wrangling a newborn, no awkward drive home afterwards, and you can stay exactly as relaxed as the massage left you.

Most of the mums I see say they wouldn't have it any other way.
