The logo

Nest & Eggs — a woven nest holding two eggs, mother and baby. Symbolic, timeless, embosses beautifully.

Clear space

Keep a margin of at least the height of one egg on all sides. Never crowd the mark with text or other elements.

Minimum size & don'ts

Mark no smaller than 22 px tall; full lockup no smaller than 120 px wide. Don't recolour, stretch, rotate, or add shadows.

04

Photography

Warm natural light, real rest, cream and skin tones. Calm over clinical — always.

the feeling we're after

Unhurried, warm, and entirely her own.

Do

  • Warm, soft natural light — morning or golden
  • Cream, white, linen and skin; calm faces, eyes resting
  • Real, in-the-moment; candles, oils, a home setting
  • Generous negative space for headlines to sit in

Avoid

  • Cold, clinical or harsh on-camera flash
  • Bright primary colours (red gym kit, blue scrubs)
  • Stocky, over-posed “spa cliché” staging
  • Heavy filters or oversaturated skin tones

07

Voice & tone

Like a trusted friend who happens to be an expert. Warm, calm, quietly reassuring.

the tone, in one line
“Everyone asks how the baby's getting on. Far fewer people ask how you're doing.”

Principles

We sound
  • Warm & unhurried
  • Plain British English
  • Gently witty
  • Spoken to her, not about a service
We don't
  • Clinical or jargon-y
  • Salesy or shouty
  • Over-promising / “luxury” clichés
  • Exclamation marks everywhere

Example — “An hour that's entirely your own. I bring everything; you just have to lie down.”
