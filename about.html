hello, I'm Kia

The someone who asks how you're doing.

I'm a massage therapist with over fifteen years of professional practice, specialising in pregnancy and postnatal care — brought to you, at home, across mid-Essex.

I'm a highly experienced and well-recommended massage therapist, trained to City & Guilds Level 3, and I've gone on to take extensive specialist training in advanced pregnancy and postnatal massage — which means I can offer treatments that are safe, effective and genuinely tailored to every stage of motherhood.

Over the years I've worked in both clinic and home-based settings, and that's given me a real understanding of what expectant and new mothers actually need. Given the choice, though, I'll always favour treating you in the comfort of your own home. You get to relax in a space that's familiar and private, you avoid the discomfort of travelling while pregnant or with a new baby, and you can enjoy the benefits of the massage without having to rush straight back out into the world afterwards.

Alongside the hands-on work, I've built up a real wealth of knowledge within the pregnancy, birth and postnatal community around Chelmsford. I care about supporting women beyond the massage itself — so I'm always happy to answer questions, share what I know, and point you towards trusted local professionals when you need them.

My aim is simple: to give you not just an excellent massage, but a warm, supportive and reassuring experience — one that leaves you feeling cared for, informed and confident, throughout your pregnancy and well beyond.
