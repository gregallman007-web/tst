Six ways to make the nest a nest.

You're right — it needs to feel woven and alive, not like a bowl. Here are six treatments: denser weaving, soft feathers tucked in, a looser rustic build, and a couple with a little chick peeking out.

All on the locked palette. Pick one (or mix — e.g. the chick from 05 in the feathered nest of 02) and I'll refine it into the final mark and rebuild the full kit.
