where I come to

Home visits across mid-Essex.

I'm based near Hartford End, between Chelmsford and Great Dunmow, and I cover a good stretch of mid-Essex from there — broadly anywhere within about a 30-minute drive. There's no travel charge across the core area; for the outer villages a small petrol fee may apply, and I'll always tell you before anything's confirmed.
