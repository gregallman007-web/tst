what I offer

Treatments, brought to your door.

Every treatment is a home visit across Chelmsford, Great Dunmow and the surrounding villages. We always start with a chat about how you're feeling and what you'd like, so the hour is shaped around you.

From 12 weeks

Pregnancy massage

A full-body treatment with a proper "pamper me" feel. We begin with you seated for a back, neck and shoulder massage, then you move onto the couch — reclined against pillows, with another under your knees to take the pressure off your lower back. From there it's feet, legs, arms, hands and scalp. By the end, most people are somewhere close to asleep.

Tends to help most with:

  • ✓Lower back, pelvic and hip pain as your posture shifts
  • ✓Sciatica, swelling, and tension across the neck and shoulders
  • ✓Broken sleep, anxiety, and the simple need for a moment's calm

£75 · 60 minutes · pregnancy-safe grapeseed oil

After birth

Postnatal massage

The weeks after birth are a strange mix — wonderful, relentless and exhausting all at once. A postnatal massage is gentle, nurturing work to help you recover, release the tension that builds up from feeding and carrying, and have a moment that's entirely your own.

Everyone asks how the baby's getting on. Far fewer people ask how you're doing.

£60 · 60 minutes · full-body, Swedish technique

Postnatal & non-pregnancy

Hot stone massage

Some weeks you don't need anything clever — you just need the stress lifted off your shoulders. Smooth, heated stones are worked through the treatment to warm the muscles and melt away tension, leaving you thoroughly unwound.

Offered postnatally and for anyone who isn't pregnant.

£75 · 50–60 minutes
