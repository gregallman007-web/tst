the area I cover

Local, in every sense.

Being so close means there's rarely a travel charge for Dunmow itself, and I know the area and its lanes well. I cover the town and the villages around it — Felsted, Little Dunmow, Barnston, Stebbing, High Easter, the Rodings and out towards Hartford End and Great Waltham. If you're tucked away down a single-track lane, that's no problem at all.
