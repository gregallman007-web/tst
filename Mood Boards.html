Take Some Time · Brand & Visual Direction

Three calm directions, and a new mark to grow into.

Before we build the site, here are three visual moods to react to — each one calm, warm and unhurried, but pulling in a slightly different way. The aim is to settle the feeling first: palette, type, imagery, the logo. Once you pick a direction (or mix bits you like from across them), I'll build the home page in that language for sign-off.

Everything here is a starting point, not a finished asset. Image areas are marked placeholders — they'll be filled with Kia's real, natural-light photography. Logos are vector concepts to choose a route, then refine.

What we're carrying forward from The Nesting Place

The old mark had real warmth — soft watercolour birds, a gentle teardrop, and a muted teal / blush / grey palette. That tenderness is worth keeping.

For Take Some Time I've kept the nurturing, natural feeling and the idea of a bird / nest / drop motif, but moved to a calmer, more grown-up palette and a cleaner type system that suits a premium, trustworthy service. Direction 03 leans most directly into that heritage.

01 Sage & Oat The brief's natural direction, refined. Herbal calm, lots of air, an elegant serif. Reads premium and reassuring — the safest, most timeless route.

What I'd like from you

This is the fork-in-the-road moment. Pick the direction that feels most like Kia's business — or tell me which bits to splice together (e.g. Direction 01's palette with Direction 03's bird mark). Once it's settled I'll build the home page in that language.

  1. Which of the three directions feels right — 01 Sage & Oat, 02 Warm Clay, or 03 Blush & Eucalyptus?
  2. Which logo route (A–F) should I refine? Should the final mark include an illustrated bird, a botanical, or stay purely typographic?
  3. Any colours, fonts or motifs to pull across from another board, or drop entirely?
  4. Confirm the type pairing for the winner — I'll lock it before building.

A few things worth deciding

  • The name treatment — lowercase & gentle (01), title case & warm (02), or all-caps & classic (03)?
  • Heritage — how much should this feel like a continuation of The Nesting Place vs. a fresh start?
  • Photography — do we have real photos of Kia, the couch set-up and treatments, or should I plan around a shoot? It changes how heavily we lean on imagery vs. type and motif.
