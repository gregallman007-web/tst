Privacy policy.

Some of what you share with us — health details especially — is personal and sensitive. This explains what we collect, why, and how it's looked after.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

Take Some Time is a sole-trader mobile massage practice run by Kia Welham, based near Hartford End, Essex. For the purposes of UK data protection law we are the "data controller" for the information you give us. You can contact us at kia@takesometime.co.uk.

2. What we collect

  • Contact details — your name, phone number, email and address, so we can arrange and carry out home visits.
  • Health information — details about your pregnancy, recovery, medical history and wellbeing, gathered through a consultation so treatments are safe and suitable. This is "special category" data and treated with extra care.
  • Booking & payment records — the treatments you've had and amounts paid (we don't store full card details).
  • Messages — the content of enquiries you send by form, WhatsApp, email or social media.

3. Why we use it, and our lawful basis

  • To arrange and provide your treatments, and to keep in touch about your bookings — on the basis of our contract with you and our legitimate interest in running the practice.
  • To make sure treatments are safe for you — your health information is used with your explicit consent, given at consultation.
  • To meet our insurance and professional record-keeping obligations — on the basis of our legal and regulatory duties.
  • To respond to your enquiries.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, and we will only send you marketing if you've asked us to.

4. How we store & protect it

Your information is kept securely and access is limited to Kia. Health and consultation records are stored confidentially and separately from general contact lists. We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your data against loss, misuse or unauthorised access.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell your information. We only share it where necessary — for example with a healthcare professional where you've asked us to, or where we're legally required to. Some service providers (such as messaging, email or payment tools) may process limited data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.

6. How long we keep it

We keep client and health records for as long as needed to provide your care and to meet our insurance and legal obligations, after which they're securely deleted or destroyed. General enquiries that don't lead to a booking are kept only as long as needed to respond.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access the information we hold about you, to have it corrected or deleted, to restrict or object to how we use it, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email kia@takesometime.co.uk and we'll respond within one month.

8. Cookies

This website uses only the cookies necessary for it to function. If we add analytics or embedded content (such as social media feeds) in future, we'll update this policy and ask for your consent where required.

9. Complaints

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your information, please tell us first so we can put it right. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
