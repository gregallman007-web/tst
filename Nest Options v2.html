Take Some Time · Nest & Eggs — the nest shape

Six nest shapes, tidied up.

Chick gone, straw gone. This round is about the form of the nest itself — where the rim sits, how deep the cup is, and how the eggs settle into it — so the silhouette reads as one clean shape.

Each adds an open back rim behind the eggs, so the nest reads as a proper cup rather than a bowl the eggs sit on top of. Pick a shape and I'll make it the final mark.

Which shape feels right?

These are all about the nest's form — pick the silhouette you like and I'll refine the weave and balance it, then rebuild the lockups, favicon and the Style Board around it.

Happy to blend — e.g. the deep cup of 01 with the cleaner rim of 02, or the airy line-work of 03 at a heavier weight.
