Frequently asked questions.

The things mums ask most often, answered. Anything not covered here — just message me, no question's too small.

Safety & timing

Is pregnancy massage safe?

Yes — pregnancy massage is widely considered safe from 12 weeks onwards when it's carried out by a qualified therapist using pregnancy-specific techniques and positioning. If you have a high-risk pregnancy, high blood pressure or any complications, have a quick word with your midwife or GP first. Massage supports your wellbeing alongside your medical care; it doesn't replace it.

When in my pregnancy can I have a massage?

From 12 weeks, right through to just after the birth.

How soon after giving birth can I have a massage?

After a straightforward birth, once you've had your postnatal check and you're feeling ready. After a caesarean or a birth with complications, wait until your GP or midwife has given you the all-clear and allow time to heal. If you're unsure, just ask.

Can I have a hot stone massage while pregnant?

No — hot stone massage isn't suitable during pregnancy, so it's offered postnatally and to anyone who isn't expecting. If you're pregnant, the pregnancy massage is the one for you.

The treatment itself

What happens during a treatment?

A 60-minute pregnancy massage starts with a seated back, neck and shoulder massage, then you settle onto the couch reclined against pillows. From there it's feet, legs, arms, hands and a finishing scalp massage. Postnatal and hot stone treatments follow a similar full-body shape, adapted to you.

What oils do you use?

Grapeseed oil is my usual for pregnancy — gentle and pregnancy-safe. There are other safe oils we can use instead if you'd prefer; just say.

What do I need to provide?

A warm bit of space and somewhere comfortable to set up. I bring the couch, towels, cushions and oils.

Can my partner be involved?

If you'd like, your partner is welcome to sit in on a pregnancy treatment and pick up a few simple techniques to help you during labour.

Area, booking & payment

Which areas do you cover?

Chelmsford, Great Dunmow, Braintree, Bishop's Stortford and the villages in between — broadly within a 30-minute drive of Hartford End. A small petrol fee may apply further out. Not sure if you're in range? Send your postcode and I'll confirm.

How do I book?

Message me on WhatsApp using the chat on the site, or fill in the contact form. I'll come back to you to talk through what you'd like and find a time. I confirm every booking personally, and I work Monday to Thursday.

What's your cancellation policy?

Please give 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Less than that may mean 50% of the treatment cost is owed, and a missed appointment without notice is charged in full.
